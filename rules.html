RULES

Last Updated: 03/12/2024

Welcome to ForoAlejandro! To ensure a positive and productive environment for all users, we have established the following forum rules. By participating in our forum, you agree to abide by these rules. Failure to comply may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the forum.

1. Respect Others

  • Treat all members with respect and courtesy. Personal attacks, harassment, or hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • Be mindful of differing opinions and engage in constructive discussions.

2. Stay On Topic

  • Each forum section is dedicated to specific topics. Please post your questions and comments in the appropriate section to keep discussions organized.
  • Off-topic posts may be removed or moved to the appropriate section by moderators.

3. No Spam or Self-Promotion

  • Do not post spam, advertisements, or promotional content without prior approval from the forum administrators.
  • Refrain from excessive posting of links to your own website, blog, or social media accounts.

4. Use Appropriate Language

  • Keep language and content appropriate for all audiences. Profanity, vulgarity, and sexually explicit content are prohibited.
  • Avoid using excessive capitalization, as it can be interpreted as shouting.

5. Protect Privacy

  • Do not share personal information about yourself or others, including addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.
  • Respect the privacy of other users and do not disclose their personal information without consent.

6. No Plagiarism

  • When sharing content, ensure that you provide proper attribution to the original source. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • If you are quoting someone else's work, please use quotation marks and provide a citation.

7. Report Issues

  • If you encounter any inappropriate behavior or content, please report it to the moderators or administrators. Do not engage in arguments or confrontations with other users.

8. Follow Moderator Instructions

  • Moderators are here to help maintain a positive environment. Please respect their decisions and follow their instructions.
  • If you have concerns about a moderator's actions, contact the forum administrators privately.

9. No Illegal Activities

  • Discussions or promotions of illegal activities, including but not limited to piracy, hacking, or drug use, are strictly prohibited.
  • Users engaging in illegal activities may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

10. Consequences of Rule Violations

  • Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the forum, depending on the severity of the offense.
  • Repeated violations may lead to more severe consequences.

11. Changes to Forum Rules

We reserve the right to modify these forum rules at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be updated accordingly. Your continued participation in the forum after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new rules.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding these forum rules, please contact us at rules@foroalejandro.com

By participating in ForoAlejandro, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by these forum rules.
